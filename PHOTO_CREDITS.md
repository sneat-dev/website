## Illustrations & generated assets

Everything else visual on the site — the hero and CTA gradient/grid fields, the
context-stack diagram, the terminal cards, the ecosystem "wall", and the Open
Graph card (`public/og/default.png`, rendered from an in-repo HTML template) —
is hand-built with CSS/SVG/HTML. No third-party illustration assets are used.

## Provenance notes

- A candidate Pexels pair-programming photo was rejected during sourcing because
  its embedded EXIF copyright disagreed with the listed author; we don't publish
  assets with uncertain provenance.
- Author names were verified against each photo's Unsplash page (linked above),
  which is the authoritative record.
